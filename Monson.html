Latter-Days President Monson

Thomas S Monson was a prophet of the Lord and was our beloved prophet until his passing when our current prophet was called President Nelson. President Monson taught many great things about being a good husband. On one occasion he taught that we should treat our wives with dignity and respect and need to love them. Abundantly Blessed April 2008 President Monson's example of listening to the spirit is also something a good father and husband should try and apply in their lives. He taught us to heed to the prompting of the Holy Ghost.

President Monson

Christ like attribute

Charity and Love: President Monson is a man full of Charity and Love toward others. Charity is the pure love of Christ. Throughout his life president Monson showed love to all those around him. This is something that makes a righteous husband and father is one who possesses Charity. One line from the Proclamation to the world that reminds me of President Monson is when it says, "Husband and wife have a solemn responsibility to love and care for each other and for their children." President Monson clearly loved his wife and children and others around him and we should strive to do the same.
